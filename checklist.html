Our Cleaning Checklists

Here's exactly what our team covers on every visit. No surprises — just a spotless home, every single time.

Standard Service

FCCC Standard Cleaning Checklist

Applies to all regular maid visits. Customized cleaning is available upon request — just ask!

Kitchen

  • Clean & sanitize all countertops
  • Clean stovetop & drip pans
  • Wipe down exterior of all appliances
  • Clean microwave inside & out
  • Wipe down cabinet fronts & handles
  • Clean & sanitize kitchen sink
  • Empty & reline trash can
  • Sweep & mop floors

Bathrooms

  • Scrub & disinfect toilet inside and out
  • Clean & sanitize sink and vanity
  • Scrub shower & tub
  • Clean all mirrors & glass
  • Wipe down all surfaces & fixtures
  • Clean shower door & tracks
  • Empty trash & replace liner
  • Sweep & mop floors

Bedrooms

  • Dust all surfaces & furniture
  • Make beds & change linens (if provided)
  • Vacuum carpets & rugs
  • Sweep & mop hard floors
  • Empty trash
  • Wipe light switches & doorknobs
  • Clean mirrors & glass surfaces

Living Areas

  • Dust all furniture & surfaces
  • Vacuum sofas, chairs & cushions
  • Vacuum carpets & area rugs
  • Sweep & mop hard floors
  • Wipe down light switches & outlets
  • Clean glass surfaces & mirrors
  • Empty trash cans

Whole Home

  • Dust ceiling fans & light fixtures
  • Remove cobwebs throughout
  • Wipe down all door frames & doors
  • Clean all interior windows & sills
  • Wipe blinds & window sills
  • All trash emptied & relined
  • Final walkthrough & quality check

Add-Ons Available

Ask about these additional services when you book:

  • Inside oven cleaning
  • Inside refrigerator cleaning
  • Inside cabinet & drawer cleaning
  • Window cleaning (inside & outside)
  • Green / eco-friendly product upgrade
  • Laundry wash & fold
Deep Clean

Move-In & Move-Out Checklist

Everything in the Standard Checklist PLUS these additional deep-clean items. Perfect for tenants wanting their deposit back — or landlords prepping for new tenants.

Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Clean inside all cabinets & drawers
  • Deep clean inside oven
  • Clean inside refrigerator & freezer
  • Degrease range hood & filter
  • Clean inside dishwasher

Bathrooms Deep Clean

  • Deep scrub all tile & grout
  • Remove soap scum & hard water stains
  • Clean inside vanity drawers & cabinets
  • Clean exhaust fans
  • Scrub caulk lines

Whole Home Extras

  • Wipe all baseboards throughout
  • Clean inside all windows + tracks
  • Wipe all doors & door frames
  • Spot clean walls & scuff marks
  • Clean all light fixtures & switch plates
  • Clean garage (if applicable)
  • Remove all cobwebs in every room
